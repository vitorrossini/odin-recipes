Carrot Cake



If you have never ate a carrot cake with chocolate topping before in your life, then where the hell were you? You are missing a lot. Next you are gonna tell me you never ate a lasagna?

This delicious vegetable sweet cake will blow your mind, and i've got the best recipe right here just for you.


Ingredients


  • 300gr of peeled carrots (cut it into chunks cause we will need to blender it);
  • 3 large eggs;
  • 1 and ¾ cups of sugar;
  • 1 cup of vegetable oil;
  • A pinch of salt;
  • 2 cups of flour;
  • 1 tablespoon of baking powder;
  • 120gr of dark chocolate;
  • 100g of crème fraiche.

Alright, let's bake this cake!


  1. Preheat the oven at 180ºC;
  2. Butter the springform pan and dust flour through it so it doesn't stick;
  3. Mix all the ingredients on the blender, put it on the springform pan and let it bake for 50 min;
  4. To make the chocolate topping, melt the chocolate with crème fraiche on lower heat and voila.

Not mandatory, but this cake is extra delicious if eaten along with a cup of coffee. Enjoy!
