Lasagna



Who doesn't love a cheesy sauced lasagna with a size of a brick, am i right?

So i will show you now how to make this delicious dish with a simple and easy recipe.


Ingredients

  • 2 packs of lasagna pasta
  • 3 glasses of ready to go sauce
  • At least 300gr of cheese (the more the marrier)
  • 500gr of grind meat
  • 300gr of ham

Seems preety easy already, right? Ok, lets make it!


  1. First thing you need to do is to drop a bit of the sauce on your baking tray, so our first layer doesnt stick on it;
  2. Preheat the oven to 200ÂºC or something like that, i don't really remember;
  3. Now we will work with layers here, so what you need to do is to cover the whole tray with a layer of lasagna pasta, then sauce, meat, cheese and ham;
  4. Repeat this until you have no more ingredients or your tray is full;
  5. Add sauce and cheese on top so we get a crusty cheese.
  6. Put it on the oven and wait until it starts to smell good (which will be probably 25 minutes);
  7. Take it out of the oven and enjoy!

That's it! I hope you liked it as much as i did.
